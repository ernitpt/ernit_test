Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Ernit ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Ernit application, available as a mobile app on Google Play and the App Store, and as a web application at ernit.app (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type What We Collect Why
Account Information Name, email address, profile photo To create and manage your account
Goal & Activity Data Goals you create, session logs, streaks, progress Core app functionality — tracking your challenges
Social Data Friend connections, feed posts, reactions, comments, motivations To enable social features (sharing progress, encouraging friends)
Payment Information Payment details are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card number. To process experience purchases
Fitness Data Activity recognition data (with your permission) To verify goal sessions via Google Fit integration
Media Photos, videos, or audio recordings you upload Session proof, profile photos, personalized hints
Usage Data App interactions, screen views, feature usage To improve the app experience
Device Information Device type, operating system, browser type To ensure compatibility and diagnose issues
Error Logs Crash reports, error messages To identify and fix bugs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To process transactions and send related information (receipts, confirmations)
  • To enable social features (friend connections, feed, reactions)
  • To send notifications about goal progress, friend activity, and reminders
  • To improve and optimize the Service
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Other Users: Your display name, profile photo, and goal progress are visible to your friends within the app. Feed posts are visible to friends you have connected with.
  • Stripe: Payment information is processed securely by Stripe. We do not have access to your full card details.
  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication, database, cloud storage, and analytics. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL. We implement Firestore Security Rules to ensure users can only access data they are authorized to view.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data via your profile settings
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data upon request
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing (e.g., fitness tracking, push notifications) via your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@ernit.app.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes.

7. Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera / Photo Library: To upload profile photos, session proof, and personalized hints
  • Microphone: To record audio messages for personalized hints
  • Activity Recognition: To track fitness activities via Google Fit for goal verification
  • Notifications: To send goal reminders, friend activity updates, and progress alerts

All permissions are optional and can be managed through your device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., experience providers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or sending an email. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
